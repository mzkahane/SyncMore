Welcome to SyncMore

Experiencing Homelessness? Backup and secure your personal information.


What is HomeMore?

The HomeMore Project delivers the Makeshift Traveler pack to individuals experiencing homelessness throughout California.
While the pack was designed to help the homeless keep their phones charged and better secure their belongings, inevitably phones and belongings get stolen, lost, or damaged.
The solution is to therefore create a mobile application to digitize personal information needed to obtain permanent housing.

What is SyncMore?

SyncMore is a cloud storage service managed under the HomeMore project. SyncMore offers:
1) A cloud storage service: Users can store and digitize important personal information as backups for physical copies in private personal drives.
2) Centralized resources: A collection of resources and support for unhoused individuals.
